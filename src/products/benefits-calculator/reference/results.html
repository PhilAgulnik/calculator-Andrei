Council Tax Reduction

£76.57 / monthly

You should not have to pay Council Tax as you qualify for full Council Tax Reduction.
You still have to pay the water and sewerage charges. If you claim Council Tax Reduction (CTR), you should also get a reduction of up to 35% on the public water and sewerage charges on your council tax bill through the Water Charges Reduction Scheme. The amount depends on how much CTR you get. You don't need to apply for this scheme, the reduction should be applied to your bill automatically with your CTR. For further information go to Scottish Water's help page about your charges.
